Welcome To getDomainAge !

This application allows you to get the age of a domain in days for a given URL.

For any assistance, please drop a mail at ljnath@ljnath.com

How to use?

  1. Login with your email as you will receive the result on your email
  2. Add a new job where you need to enter the URLs for which you need the age. Input URLs can be comma or new-line seperated
  3. Wait for your job to be processed. Once processed, you will receive an email with the domain age for each URL.
